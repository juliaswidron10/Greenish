BEING (almost)
VEGAN

Have you ever thought about going plant based? Maybe you stumbled onto and article about the health benefits. Maybe you’ve even tried doing it for a bit. Since you clicked on to this tab, we can assume that you have at least considered it. You don’t have to become completely plant based to make a difference in the world even something as a meat free Monday can make a huge difference. What’s important is to find a formula that works from you. Otherwise you should check out these plant-based friendly cafes and restaurants below.
